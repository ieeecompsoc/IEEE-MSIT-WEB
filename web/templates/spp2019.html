IEEE MSIT brings SPP 2019

IEEE MSIT Student Branch is excited to launch its SPP Program for 2019 Summer Break from 5th June - 20th July 2019.

Let's Learn something New!

SPP is a mentorship program where talented and skilled Mentors whether a Student, Professional or a Faculty can lead a team of Mentees through the path of learning something new and bring out the innovation in them. Register from below Links:

Register as a Mentor

Register yourself as a Mentor: https://bit.ly/sppmentor19

Register your Idea

Register your ideas that you would like to work on as a Mentee: https://bit.ly/sppidea19

Event Timeline

Registration Deadline

23rd May 2019, Thursday

Notification to Mentors & Mentee

23rd May - 24th May 2019
We will make sure to select skilled mentors from the Applications we will receive and approve their proposed projects.

Linking of Mentor - Mentee

31st May 2019
After shortlisting the Mentors for dedicated Domains, we will then map the Domain of Projects provided by Mentees with Mentor who can guide through the Event Summer Program Time Period.

Time Period for Development of Project to Product

5th June 2019 - 20th July 2019
6 Weeks of Summer Period will be allocated to Teams with proper Skilled Mentor for the completion of Project to Product.

Steps for Development

1

Register your Idea

Register your Idea on the Link given above which must be related to one real-world problem or have some dedicated applications which will help us to select your Idea more quickly.

2

Build your Project to Product

Build your Project to Product with the support and guidance of Mentor allotted to you or have been allotted from your side in Registration Form.

3

Selected Projects will get Funding

After the 6 Weeks of Development, the completed and fully functional projects will be funded by IEEE MSIT for upcoming journey for deployment in Society.
