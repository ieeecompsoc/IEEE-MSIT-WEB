About

A little about how and why we do this.

Vision

The quest of Technical Project Exhibition 2019 is to provide a launching pad to all the future engineering superstars to showcase their innovative and unique projects in front of an esteemed panel of judges and peers from all over DELHI.

Project Domains

This technical extravaganza witness projects from various tech-domains like Digital Image Processing (MATLAB), Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, Android App Development and wireless applications, IoT projects, signal processing and VLSI projects.

Impact

The magnificent event organized by IEEE MSIT provides a platform to exceptional ideas and creativity. Every project idea is aimed at creating solutions for a challenging future ahead. One of the major objectives of TPE is to develop affordable technologies that creates an overall positive impact on the society.

Levels

Level 1

Ascent

All the aspiring participants must upload a brief descriptive account of their respective projects at Registation link by 5th March 2020. The abstracts will be screened by the honourable judges and the selected entries will be invited for the succeeding rounds.
